Miyuki is arguably the most important unit in the game. Her passive ability reduces the Rage of all enemies whenever she attacks. Since enemies can only use their devastating Ultimate skills when their Rage bar is full, Miyuki effectively silences the entire enemy board.

Constructing a team requires more than just picking your ten strongest SSRs. You must build around your **Leader's Passives**. Each Leader (starting with the Swordsman) has specific conditions that, when met, grant massive buffs like +15% ATK or +20% HP to the entire team.

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Check Passive Requirements">
    Look at the Leader's "Skill" tab. For example, the Nightmare Hunter may require you to have at least 5 Attack or Mage units with an average energy cost below 14.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Slot Your Core Radiants">
    Add your "Must-Haves": Miyuki for control, a Guardian (Selena/Kiraya), and a Healer (Anna/Polly).

  <Step num="4" title="Fill Synergy Gaps">

  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Test in Trials">
    Run a few stages to see if your energy generation is too slow or if your frontline collapses too quickly. Adjust the ratio of Guardians to Attackers accordingly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Rerolling allows you to start the game with a powerful foundation. By using a guest account, you can quickly reset your progress if your initial summons do not include key SSR characters.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Create Guest Account">
    Launch the game and select the guest login option to avoid linking your main email immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear Tutorial">
    Complete the opening chapters to unlock the summon menu and receive your first batch of free tickets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Claim All Rewards">
    Navigate to the mailbox and event tabs to collect pre-registration bonuses and launch rewards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Summons">
    Spend your free tickets on the Eternal Summon banner to secure your first SSR Radiants.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Evaluate & Reset">
    If you did not pull at least two S-Tier or A-Tier units, log out, clear game data, and repeat the process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

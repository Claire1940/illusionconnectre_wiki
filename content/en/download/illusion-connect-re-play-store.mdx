<Section icon={Target} title="Reroll Strategy for Beginners">

Rerolling is a common practice in Illusion Connect: Re to ensure your account starts with at least one top-tier SSR character. By using a guest account, you can quickly reset your progress if your initial summons do not meet your expectations.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initialize Guest Account">
    Start the game as a guest to avoid binding your primary email until you are satisfied with your pulls.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Complete Tutorial">
    Finish the opening story and tutorial chapters to unlock the summon menu.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Claim All Rewards">
    Collect pre-registration milestones and launch bonuses from your mailbox.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Summons">
    Use your free tickets on the Eternal Summon banner, then transition to the Selected Rate-Up banner.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Bind Account">
    Once you secure your desired SSR Radiants, link your account to save your progress permanently.
  </Step>
</Steps>

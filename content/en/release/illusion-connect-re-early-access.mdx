<Section icon={Shield} title="Combat Mechanics: Master the 3x3 Grid">

The tactical heart of **illusion connect re early access** is the 3x3 grid. Unlike idle RPGs, you must manually time your deployments. Each Radiant has a unique "Entry Skill" that triggers the moment they hit the board. Learning when to drop a healer or a tank can mean the difference between victory and defeat.

**Key Combat Concepts:**
- **Deployment Cost**: You gain energy over time. High-tier units cost 15-20, while low-tier units cost 8-12.
- **Leader Skills**: Your main avatar has skills that trigger based on the composition of your team (e.g., having 5 Sorcerers unlocks a damage buff).
- **Class Counters**: Attackers deal more damage to Sorcerers, while Sorcerers excel at clearing multiple enemies.
- **Rage Management**: Characters gain Rage by attacking or taking damage. Once full, they use their Ultimate Skill.

<Callout type="info" title="Tactical Advice">
Don't deploy your units all at once. If the enemy is about to use a massive AoE attack, wait until after the hit to deploy your Healer so their entry skill restores the team's HP effectively.
</Callout>

</Section>
